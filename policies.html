Delivery, Exchange & Return Policies

Delivery Policy

Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): Free on orders over $50, otherwise $9.99
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days): $19.99
  • Overnight Shipping (1 business day): $39.99

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Delivery Timeframes

Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on your location and product availability. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

International Shipping

We currently ship to select international destinations. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Customs fees and import duties are the responsibility of the customer.

Return Policy

30-Day Return Window

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items. Products must be in their original condition, unused, and in original packaging with all tags attached.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Customized or personalized products
  • Final sale items (clearly marked at checkout)
  • Opened candles or fragrances
  • Items damaged due to misuse or normal wear

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact our customer service at hello@luxeliving.com with your order number
  2. Receive a Return Authorization (RA) number and instructions
  3. Package the item securely with all original materials
  4. Ship to the address provided using a trackable method

Return Shipping Costs

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error (defective item, wrong item shipped, etc.).

Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect your return. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Exchange Policy

Product Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product, please follow these steps:

  1. Initiate a return following the process above
  2. Once your return is received, place a new order for the desired item
  3. For size/color exchanges of the same product, contact us for expedited processing

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact us immediately at hello@luxeliving.com with photos. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no cost to you, including return shipping.

Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After this window, orders enter our processing system and cannot be cancelled. Please contact us immediately if you need to cancel an order.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our delivery, exchange, or return policies, please contact our customer service team at hello@luxeliving.com or call (555) 123-4567.
