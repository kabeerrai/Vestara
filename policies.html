Delivery & Returns Policies

1. Shipping & Delivery

We offer standard and express shipping options. All in-stock orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Shipping times are estimated as follows:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 days): RS.250.00 (Free for orders over RS.5000)
  • Express Shipping (1-2 days): RS.450.00

A tracking number will be provided once your order ships.

2. Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns or exchanges of unused and undamaged items within 30 days of delivery. The item must be in its original packaging. Please note that personalized or engraved items are final sale and cannot be returned.

To initiate a return, please contact us at hello@vestara.com with your order number.

3. Warranty & Repairs

All Vestara jewelry comes with a 6-month warranty covering manufacturing defects. This excludes damage caused by misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear.

If you have any questions about our delivery, exchange, or return policies, please contact our customer service team at hello@vestara.com or call (555) 123-4567.
