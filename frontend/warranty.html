Warranty Information

Product Warranty

At Vestara, we stand behind the quality of our products. All items purchased from our store come with a comprehensive warranty to ensure your satisfaction and peace of mind.

Coverage Period

Our standard warranty covers manufacturing defects and material flaws for the following periods:

  • Furniture items: 2 years from date of purchase
  • Lighting fixtures: 1 year from date of purchase
  • Decorative accessories: 6 months from date of purchase
  • Textiles and soft furnishings: 90 days from date of purchase

What's Covered

Our warranty covers:

  • Manufacturing defects in materials or workmanship
  • Structural failures under normal use
  • Hardware malfunctions (for applicable products)
  • Finish deterioration not caused by misuse

What's Not Covered

The warranty does not cover:

  • Normal wear and tear from regular use
  • Damage caused by accidents, misuse, or negligence
  • Modifications or repairs made by unauthorized parties
  • Damage from improper cleaning or maintenance
  • Products purchased from unauthorized retailers
  • Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect functionality

How to Make a Warranty Claim

If you believe your product has a defect covered by warranty:

  1. Contact our customer service team at hello@luxeliving.com
  2. Provide your order number and photos of the defect
  3. Our team will review your claim within 3-5 business days
  4. If approved, we'll arrange for repair, replacement, or refund

Extended Warranty

For added protection, we offer extended warranty options at the time of purchase. Contact our sales team for more information about extended coverage plans.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our warranty policy, please don't hesitate to contact us at hello@luxeliving.com or call (555) 123-4567.
